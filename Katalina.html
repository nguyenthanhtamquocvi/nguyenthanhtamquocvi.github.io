Katalina

I wield this sword to

protect those dear to me.

A former knight of the Erste Empire. Her strong sense of justice led her to betray the empire and free Lyria, the very prisoner she was charged with guarding. Now, having become a pillar of strength for the entire crew, she is both Gran and Lyria's greatest supporter —and Vyrn's greatest admirer.

She employs skillful swordplay and a magic blade in her fighting style, utilizing techniques that are peerless in both beauty and strength. When it comes to battle, there are few situations she can't handle.
